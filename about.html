Luxury Lychee Farms

Luxury Lychee Farms was founded in 1952 by Kaimana and Groff Lee in central Hawaii. They began as small farmers with just a few plants. After introducing this fruit to the locals, everyone found a true love of this plant. Most of the families in their area were fairly poverished and the Lees wanted to help support it's community and found oppurtunity in this new beloved fruit. By 1971 they had grown exponentially and employed 76% of their region. Although the farming was gruelling work they made sure to take care of their employees. With excellent compensation, extensive vacation, maternal leave just to say a few these employees became family. Now with the same idealisms they always have, they have expanded to Vietnam as well.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide and produce the freshest, juciest and healthiest products. We strive to see our customers smile with satisfaction. We want to continue to grow and stay the biggest lychee coorperation all while taking care of our family and employees. We eventually want to extend to Japan and other parts of the states. We also want to create even more delicious recipies and canned goods.

Workers = Family

We consider our employees as our family. We know you hear this phrase a lot in the work force and most of the time it is not true. However, here at Luxury Lychee we do actually care. Unlike other coorperations that require extensive physical labor we provide the best compensation anyone can ask for. What our employees need is work-life balance and we make sure that it is provided. 40 hour work weeks, always voluntary (never required) time and half paid overtime, benefits, maternal leave, quarterly bonuses and much more.
